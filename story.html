The Taste of Turkey Around the World

DEGIRMEN, which means "mill" in Turkish, is a symbol of hard work, movement, and creating something genuine. We put special meaning into this image: just as a mill transforms grain into aromatic bread, we combine the best traditions of Turkish cuisine with the modern rhythm of life, creating dishes that you want to enjoy again and again.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make delicious and accessible products and dishes in which care and soul are felt. We want every guest who enters DEGIRMEN to feel warmth, like at home.
